It's a maths based puzzle game on available on Google Play: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.simplyapped.calculate

You choose from a set of cards numbered 1 to 9, 25, 50, 75, and 100, Then, using only plus, minus, multiply and divide, try and get to the randomly chosen total.
There is ALWAYS a solution! Even when sometimes it doesn't feel like it...

NO Adverts.
In-App Purchases: Not needed to play or to complete the game, but only to view a solution. You have ten free solutions to start with.
